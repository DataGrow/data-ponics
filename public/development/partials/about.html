About dataPonics

dataPonics is a group project to demonstrate the web development skills aquired by four teammates while attending DevMountain's Web Development Immersive.

We utilized the development technologies of the MEAN stack (MongoDB, ExpressJS, Angular, and NodeJS. In addition, we implemented a RasperryPi computer board with accompanied sensors to track data points such as temperature, humidity, and light of a hydroponic plant apparatus. This setup has allowed us to collect data stored on a cloud(MongoLab) that is displayed on the client utilizing d3.js chart technology.
